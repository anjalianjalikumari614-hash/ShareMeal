About Our Mission

ShareMeal is a community-driven platform designed to bridge the gap between food surplus and hunger. We believe that no edible food should go to waste when there are people in need.

By connecting hotels, restaurants, and event organizers with local NGOs, we facilitate the timely recovery and distribution of surplus food.
